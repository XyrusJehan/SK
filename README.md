📖 Overview

The Budget Expenditure and Requirements Monitoring System with Public Portal is a centralized web-based platform designed to streamline the submission, monitoring, and public disclosure of SK financial and planning documents across barangays.

It empowers:

🧑‍💼 SK Officials – to create and submit reports
🏛️ LYDO & SK Federation President – to monitor compliance
🌍 Residents – to access public records and provide feedback

This system promotes transparency, accountability, and youth participation in governance.
